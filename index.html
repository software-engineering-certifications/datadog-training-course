What is Datadog?

Datadog is one type of monitoring and analytics tool for information technology (IT) and DevOps teams that can be used to determine the performance metrics as well as event monitoring for infrastructure and cloud services. This software can easily monitor services such as servers, databases, and tools. This tool is the best for monitoring service for cloud-scale applications, providing monitoring of servers, databases, tools, and services, through a SaaS-based data analytics platform.

How Datadog tool is helpful?

In these reason Datadog tool is helpful:-

  • 1. Datadog is reasonably priced with a low barrier to entry.
  • 2. Datadog is highly active in its community-building efforts and maintains a repository of APIs, libraries, and community contributions in support of its platform.
  • 3. Datadog is widely used among both startups and enterprises, and their customer lists certainly reflect this broad range.
  • 4. Datadog monitoring/graphing interface is very flexible in allowing you to display complex graphs with zero coding.
